Download Sources

Follow from point 0 to 4 here and then, continue here. For download the sources 

$ mkdir ~/your_folder 
$ cd ~/your_folder
$ repo init -u git://github.com/J-Team/platform_manifest.git -b jb-mr2
$ repo sync
To update the changes 

$ repo sync
again

Build the sources

To build the source type these in Terminal: 

$ . build/envsetup.sh
$ lunch ...
$ make -j*
Replace * with numbers of cores of your PC

Rebuild

To rebuild the source after some edits, write this in your Terminal: 

$ . build/envsetup.sh
$ lunch ...
$ make clean
$ make -j* bacon
Replace * with numbers of cores of your PC
